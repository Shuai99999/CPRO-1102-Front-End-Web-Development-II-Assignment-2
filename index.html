FAQ

How often should I change the water?

It's best to change about 10–20% of the water weekly to keep it clean and maintain a healthy environment for your aquatic life.

Do I need special soil for plants?

Using nutrient-rich substrate designed for aquatic plants helps them grow stronger and healthier, though some hardy plants can survive in regular gravel.

Can fish and shrimp live together?

Yes, many small fish and shrimp can coexist, but make sure the fish aren’t aggressive and provide plenty of hiding spots for the shrimp.
